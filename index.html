HOW TO SET UP A PROJECTOR

WITH IMAGES

1. FINDING THE RIGHT LOCATION

It's very important when setting up a projector that you consider the location you are putting it in.
A projector works best in dark rooms and should preferably be projecting against a flat white wall.
If you are willing to spend a bit of extra money on a screen to project onto, it can increase image quality and brightness , so it is highly recommended

2. FINDING THE RIGHT HEIGHT

There are a few ways to mount a projector, with the most common being:

  • Mounted on a table in front of the user
  • Mounted high on the wall behind the user
  • Mounted hanging from the ceiling

The simplest and safest way of mounting is on a table in front, so this is the method we recommend.
Generally the height that the projector is positioned at is the same height as the bottom of the projection (assuming it is flat on a table)

3. PLUGGING IT IN

There is likely only 3 potential cables you would need to plug into your projector.

  1. The power cable for the projector
  2. The cable for a disk reader if you want one
  3. The HDMI cable from your laptop or desktop
